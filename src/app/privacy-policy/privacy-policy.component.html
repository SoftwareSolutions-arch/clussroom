Clussroom is just like a.. typical Classroom

Conducting classes should be simple. Because teachers impart precious knowledge, time should not be used to navigate the intricacies of adopting a new tool to teach.

Clussroom is easy to use and it further relieves the administrative responsibilities of classes and teaching to other parties, if they like to. Even if they take up such administrative tasks, handling them is easy and straightforward.

Clussroom uses a transparent approach to encourage participation, and an easy and direct way to allow learners understand how they can participate and ultimately contribute to the overall grades which they hope to achieve.

We understand that visual sessions are an important part of learning and transmission, and this forms part of the range of tools which teachers can use to conduct classes.
